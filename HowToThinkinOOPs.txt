Object Oriented Programming when you want to map real-life objects and their associated behaviour.

Example : You are going to design the automatic control system of a car

Step 1 : Identify all the objects in the problem context.
Step 2 : Identify the properties or data associated. 
Step 3 : Identify the operations that can be performed on the object. 
Step 4 : Bundle them together and you have your class.
